Getting Started


How do I use the app?

Begin by navigating to 'Input Lifestyle' on the home page. Input values and click submit!

Then head to breakdown to see your footprint calculation.

There is one saved calculation available per month, so enough time is given for the improvements you make to take effect in reducing your emissions!

How do I use find my energy usage- I'm not familiar with 'kwh'?

The term kwh stands for 'kilowatt hours'.

This measurement is used for measuring consumption of heating and electricity, and as such will be available on your monthly bill.

Why do I need to enter my income?

Income is collected to tailor improvements based on what you can realistically afford to invest in living a greener life.

This value can be approximated if you do not want to enter your income. We do not store this date for anything beyond suggesting improvements.

How are the calculations performed?

The calculation involves multiplying the values you provide in using something which produced CO2 emissions, and multiplying by a 'conversion factor'. This value is then an individual emission factor.

These can be summed to get a total emission value. If the right underlying emissions inputs are chosen (like we have tried to choose!), this emission value can be said to be a carbon footprint.

Conversion Factors are available from the UK Gov Website.

Do you use any external APIs?

We use OpenAI's model API. So sometimes there may be a delay when fetching the breakdown!
